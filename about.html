ABOUT US

Tespaul Global Immigration Services was founded by Omololu Nick Apata. He is a Regulated Canadian immigration Consultant (RCIC) in good standing with the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC). An immigrant himself, Omololu has worked as a classroom teacher for many years in Canada before becoming an immigration consultant, a decision borne out of his passion for immigrants and the complex immigration processes they face in their quest to come to Canada either as visitors or permanent immigrants. He is especially interested in working with and advocating for refugees who might have been forced out of their places of permanent residence due to war or religious conflicts.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to work proudly as an immigration consultant firm through transparent honesty and utmost professionalism. Through diligence and thoroughness, we hope to guide our clients along pathways that best define and meet their various immigration needs. Our vision is to set a standard for best practices in the immigration industry through advocacy and result-oriented representation.
